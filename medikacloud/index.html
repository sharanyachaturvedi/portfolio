The AI

The model behind the product

Hospital demand isn't seasonal — it's irregular and life-critical. That's what made it hard:

Steady e.g. gloves, saline

Predictable. Classic forecasting works.

Lumpy e.g. implants

Quiet, then a spike. Smoothing models miss exactly what matters.

Erratic e.g. emergency drugs

No pattern at all. Needs per-SKU logic.

Three iterations to get it right

✕

Prophet

Great at seasonality — smoothed away the lumpy spikes that mattered.

✕

XGBoost

Caught non-linear patterns — couldn't scale across 1,500+ SKUs.

✓

LightGBM

Fast and accurate on sparse, high-cardinality demand. Shipped.

What the model sees → what the hospital gets

Demand type

Steady / Lumpy / Erratic — different logic per pattern

Criticality tier

Life-saving SKUs get zero stockout tolerance

Geo layer

Weather + regional procedure trends — coastal ≠ North ≠ South
