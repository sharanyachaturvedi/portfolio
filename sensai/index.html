The Problem

Wearable Fatigue

Sleep tracking is dominated by Fitbit, Oura, Apple Watch. Every one requires something on your body.

The Insight

Put the tracker in the bed.

If the mattress itself is the sensor, there's nothing to wear, charge, or remember. Zero friction. 100% passive.

The Problem · Hardware

Hard or Soft. Never Both.

The physical mattress is broken — you could buy a hard mattress or a soft one, never one that adjusted to your body. No half-and-half, no way to change your mind as per your body.

The Opportunity

Brand to Tech Company

The Sleep Company wanted to lead the category — that meant moving into technology, building for the future. SensAI was that: a new product category, a new revenue model, and a new position as a tech-first wellness brand.

The product suite

Two connected hardware products. One unified mobile experience.

PM Win #1 · Applied ML

The Proprietary Sleep Score

Four biometric streams, one number: 0–100. The hard part was defining what it means — and what to do about it.

HRV + respiratory rate + movement + sleep stage data synthesised into one trusted number

Ran 50+ user interviews and A/B tests to iterate on how the score is displayed and explained

Reduced false movement alerts by 40% through iterative firmware and app co-tuning

PM Win #2 · GenAI

Your Personal Sleep Coach

Yesterday vs today comparison — and a weekly trend view of your sleep patterns

Conversational: "You sleep 18 minutes less on weeknights — here's why that matters"
